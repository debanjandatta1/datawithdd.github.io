Hi. I'm Debanjan.

Welcome to my data journey.

I have been associated with data for the past 11 years. It started with me doing Statistics as part of my Economics course in 2013. Since then, I have completed a bachelors and masters in Economics.

I entered the corporate world as a Content Analyst at a digital marketing firm where I analysed client data to drive business value and insights for the first time. After spending more than a year in the role, I moved to HSBC Bank,as a Decision Science Analyst-Model Monitoring in the Risk and Compliance team. I spent 2 years in this role, monitoring ML models that helped mitigate the risk that such models posed periodically.

In my 3.5 years of work experience, I felt the need to understand the world of data science much better and explore domains that I had not yet. This is why, I am now pursuing a Business Analytics masters program at Singapore Management University.

Currently, I am exploring big data, cloud technologies and model deployment platforms.
