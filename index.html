Hi. I'm Debanjan.

Welcome to my website.

My main motivation behind creating this was because I felt like documenting my data science journey from the past 8 years in one place. I completed my bachelors and masters as an Economics student who was studying Statistics and Econometrics on the side. I stepped into the corporate world as a Content Analyst where I helped clients understand their online presence and drew strategies to help them optimize their website traffic and sales.

After spending a little more than a year, I moved to HSBC Bank, this time as a Decision Science Analyst-Model Monitoring in the Risk and Compliance team. I spent 2 years in this role, monitoring machine learning models that helped model owners and developers understand and mitigate the risk that such models posed periodically. My experience in both these roles led me to gain a hands-on experience in tools such as Python, SQL, Tableau, QlikSense, Google Analytics apart concepts such as User Acceptance Testing(UAT), statistical modelling, machine learning, dashboarding etc. In these 3.5 years, I have worked in two organisations of entirely different domains, sizes and culture. But in both, I have contributed strongly in my roles through soft skills such as clear and concise communication, effective stakeholder management and timely delivery.

Currently, I am pursuing a Business Analytics masters program at Singapore Management University where my selected courses include a mix of analytics tools and concepts, financial domain knowledge as well as model deployment and big data management tools.

My portfolio includes a mix of domains,tools and skills and they reflect my learnings over the past several years.

Happy browsing!
